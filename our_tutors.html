Meet Our Tutors



Founder and President: Niko Laskaris

Niko grew up in the Mad River Valley, graduating from Harwood Union High School in 2011. While in high school, he taught himself to take the SAT and the SAT II subject tests and five years later graduated from Yale University, with distinction in his major. Prior to founding Long Trail Prep, Niko spent three years at Yale working as a Science and Quantitative Reasoning tutor at the Yale Center for Teaching and Learning, focusing primarily on Calculus and First-Order Logic.

Senior Tutor: Rob

Based in Vermont’s Mad River Valley, Rob Williams, Ph.D. teaches history, global studies, media, communications, marketing, and journalism at the University of Vermont (UVM) and Champlain College. With a B.A. in history from Princeton University and an M.A. and Ph.D. from the University of New Mexico, Rob is a 30 year veteran of classroom teaching and learning, and taught Advanced Placement (AP) college prep courses in U.S., European, and world history at Albuquerque Academy for many years prior to his return to New England to pursue university teaching and research. Rob’s side hustles include running the YakItToMe! mobile BBQ food wagon, performing “polk phunk” music with the Phineas Gage Project, and publishing Vermont Independent, an online news journal.

If you'd like to learn more about Rob's tutoring availability, please inquire on our contact page.
