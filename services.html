Tutoring Services

Not all tutoring agencies are created equal. Long Trail Prep believes in the power of personal connection, and we work only individually with our students. Our tutors get to know their pupils one-on-one, and tailor teaching sessions holistically to meet their specific needs. By the end of the process, our tutors become mentors — rather than just teachers — to their students.

Standardized Test Prep

For standardized exams, success can be as much about understanding the test as about finding the right answer. Long Trail Prep works with families to help select standardized exams most suited to students' skills and abilities. We then train our students to think like the tests they are taking. Our strategies enable them to approach each section of their exams with confidence, to unravel questions deliberately, and to select the best answers efficiently.

We offer proprietary standardized test preparation for the following exams:

  • SAT
  • SAT II Subject Tests
  • ACT
  • PSAT
  • SSAT

College Admissions

Long Trail Prep's college preparation mission is simple: to guide families through every step of the college admissions process. All of our college admissions tutors are recent graduates of Ivy League and other selective colleges. Not so long ago, they themselves applied — with remarkable success — to the best American institutes of higher education. They work with students on every aspect of their applications.

Academic tutoring

Long Trail Prep are specialists in a range of disciplines, and hold degrees in the humanities, social sciences, and pure sciences. We tutor all subjects at the primary and secondary school level. Whether a student needs consistent reinforcement in a particular subject area, help reviewing for an exam, or a guiding hand for writing a well-constructed essay, our tutors are experts at every step of the process.

We offer tutoring services for all AP courses.

Special Tutoring Services

While Long Trail Prep's network of recent graduates from Ivy League and other top universities provides exceptional tutoring services, sometimes an even greater degree of expertise and experience is requested from our clients. Long Trail Prep also provides clients with access to individualized lessons with current University Professors and PhDs from the University of Vermont for a truly life-changing educational experience. These tutors are published authors, researchers, and academics with decades of experience in their respective fields.

Special Tutoring Services are billed at a higher rate than our other tutoring services. If you are interested in learning more about Special Tutoring Services, please inquire on our contact page.
